Lanyu
(Lori) Xu

I design, analyze, and build efficient edge AI systems — where intelligent perception runs close to the data, on the hardware that matters. My work spans vision transformer efficiency and edge-optimized inference, applied to autonomous driving, medical imaging, and AI-powered fitness and sports analysis.

Ph.D. in Computer Science, Wayne State University (advised by Dr. Weisong Shi). B.S. in Computer Science, Tongji University.

Edge Intelligent System Lab

The Edge Intelligent System Lab is motivated by enabling intelligent technologies to be performed at the edge of the network — where computation happens in proximity to data sources. We focus on resource-constrained hardware and computation-efficient software co-design, optimizing performance and efficiency for edge intelligent systems.
