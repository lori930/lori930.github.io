Lanyu (Lori) Xu

Lanyu is an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science and Engineering Department, Oakland University, Michigan, where she work on designing, analyzing, and implementing heterogeneous, efficient edge intelligent systems. She got her Ph.D. degree in Computer Science Department at Wayne State University under the supervision of Dr. Weisong Shi. Before that, she did her bachelors in Computer Science Department, Tongji University.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Linkedin

Office: 115 Library Drive, Engineering Center, Room 524

Edge Intelligent System Lab

Edge Intelligent System Lab is motivated by enabling intelligent technologies to be performed at the edge of the network, where computation happens in the proximity of data sources. Our lab is dedicated to resource-constrained hardware and computation-efficient software co-design, seeking the optimization of performance and efficiency for edge intelligent systems.
